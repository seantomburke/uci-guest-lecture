Industry Trends

[Industry trends and AI revolution overview]

AI Revolution & Technology Trends

Current Technology Landscape

  • 🤖 AI-assisted development is now the norm
  • 🧠 AI Engineering as a new discipline
  • ⚛️ Frontend trends: React Server Components
  • 🥞 Full-stack expectations for all engineers
  • 🎨 Frontend, Backend, DevOps, Design convergence
  • 📚 The importance of continuous learning

The Leveling Effect

  • 🚀 Junior developers can write senior-level code with AI assistance
  • ⚡ Learning new technologies is faster than ever
  • ⚠️ But basic coding skills are now table stakes
  • 🧩 Problem-solving and system design become differentiators

Industry Changes & Impact

Current Industry Trends

  • 💻 Interviews are changing, coding interviews evolving
  • 🏢 In Person vs fully remote work
  • 🤖 AI usage within companies
  • 🔧 Go, Python, Rust gaining popularity
  • 🐳 DevOps: Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, Cloud Architects
  • 📉 Less engineers to do the same amount of work

Good or Bad for New Grads?

  • 📈 More expectations - not just frontend or backend, Fullstack
  • ⬆️ With less Engineers required, the bar will rise
  • 🏆 Those willing to learn and adapt will win
  • 🤖 A new grad with AI can achieve what a Senior engineer could achieve
  • 👨‍🏫 Opportunity to teach industry professionals

Modern Interview Processes

GoLinks Interview Numbers

  • 📊 2594 applicants → 313 screened → 210 invited to quiz → 30 passed quiz → 20 projects → 7 selected
  • 💻 Technical assessments (Hacker Rank, no AI)
  • 🎮 Project-based challenges (e.g., Wordle clone) AI allowed
  • 🔄 Adaptation exercises (with AI allowed)

What to Expect Generally

  • 💻 Might still do Hacker rank tests, prep for it
  • 🤖 May or may not allow AI, prep for both
  • ❓ Ask the recruiter what their policy on AI is
  • 📝 Always ask for feedback
  • 🖊️ Could be whiteboard coding in person

Resume Filter - What Gets You Filtered Out

Common Resume Mistakes

  • 📸 No profile picture, no attention to detail
  • 📖 No story
  • 💻 No Github on Resume
  • 🚫 Github isn't filled out
  • 📁 No projects on Github
  • 🌐 No personal portfolio

What Hiring Managers Want

  • 🧩 Problem-solving ability over memorized algorithms
  • 🔄 Adaptability in uncertain situations
  • 💬 Communication skills - can you explain your thinking?
  • 🌱 Growth mindset - how do you handle feedback?
  • 🤝 Cultural fit - will you thrive in our environment?

Creator vs Consumer - The 80/20 Rule

The Divide

  • 📊 20% of a marketplace are Creators
  • 👀 80% of a marketplace are Consumers

Consumers

  • ⏳ Wait for content to come to them
  • 💼 Wait for jobs to come to them
  • 📧 Wait to hear back from applications
  • 🚫 Stagnant

Creators

  • 📝 Make Content (Blogs, Videos, Apps)
  • 🤝 Make connections
  • 🎯 Get involved
  • 🌍 Share creations
  • 📈 Continuously growing
