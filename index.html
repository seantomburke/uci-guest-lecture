Industry Changes & Impact

Current Industry Trends

  • 💻 Interviews are changing, coding interviews evolving
  • 🏢 In Person vs fully remote work
  • 🤖 AI usage within companies
  • 📉 Less engineers to do the same amount of work
  • 🗣️ "All code will be written by AI in 2026"

Good or Bad for New Grads?

  • 📈 More expectations - not just frontend or backend, Fullstack
  • ⬆️ With less Engineers required, the bar will rise
  • 🏆 Those willing to learn and adapt will win
  • 🤖 It's possible for a new grad assisted with AI to perform Senior level engineering work
  • 🎓 With new AI concepts weekly, anyone can spend time to become an expert
  • 👨‍🏫 Coding ≠ Engineering, Engineers will still be needed

Interview Funnel Reality

The Reality

  • 🎯 Only 1 in 370 applicants gets selected
  • 📊 Most filtering happens at the resume stage
  • 💪 Persistence and preparation are key
  • 🔄 Each rejection is practice for the next opportunity

Modern Interview Process Is Changing

GoLinks Interview Stages

  • 📋 Resume screening and initial review
  • 💻 Technical assessments (HackerRank) No AI allowed
  • 🎮 Project-based challenges (e.g., Wordle clone) AI Allowed
  • 🔄 Live coding or adaptation exercises, AI Allowed

What to Expect & How to Prepare

  • 💻 Companies might still do Leetcode tests
  • 🖊️ Could be whiteboard coding in person
  • 🤖 May or may not allow AI - prepare for both scenarios
  • ❓ Always ask the recruiter about their AI policy
  • 📝 Always ask for feedback after each stage
  • 🎯 Practice explaining your thought process clearly
  • 🔄 Be ready to adapt and iterate on solutions

How to Get a Job

Resume Filters

Common Resume/Portfolio Mistakes

  • 📸 No profile picture, attention to detail
  • 📖 No story on Portfolio
  • 💻 No Github on Resume
  • 🌎 No Linkedin
  • 🚫 Github isn't filled out
  • 📁 No projects on Github
  • 🌐 No personal portfolio
  • 🔨 No Projects

What Hiring Managers Want

  • 🧩 Problem-solving ability over memorized algorithms
  • 🔄 Adaptability in uncertain situations
  • 💬 Communication skills - can you explain your thinking?
  • 🌱 Growth mindset - how do you handle feedback?
  • 🤝 Cultural fit - will you thrive in the environment?

Creator vs Consumer - The 80/20 Rule

The Divide

  • 📊 20% of a marketplace are Creators
  • 👀 80% of a marketplace are Consumers

Consumers

  • ⏳ Wait for content to come to them
  • 💼 Wait for jobs to come to them
  • 📧 Wait to hear back from applications
  • 🚫 Stagnant

Creators

  • 📝 Make Content (Blogs, Videos, Apps)
  • 🤝 Make connections
  • 🎯 Get involved
  • 🌍 Share creations
  • 📈 Continuously growing
